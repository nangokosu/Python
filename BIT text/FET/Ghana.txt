Investments of nationals and companies of each Contracting Party shall be accorded fair and equitable treatment and full protection and security in the territory of the other Contracting Party. 
It is understood that the concepts of “fair and equitable treatment” and “full protection and security” means treatment that meets the standard required by customary international law and does not require treatment in addition to, or beyond such a standard. 
Neither Contracting Party shall, in any way, impair by unreasonable or discriminatory measures, the management, maintenance, use, enjoyment or disposal of investments in its territory of nationals or companies of the other Contracting Party.
